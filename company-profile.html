Leading with Innovation, Philanthropy, and Environmental Sustainability

IAR Ballscrews (previously known as Institute of Applied Research) is an organisation that goes beyond manufacturing and business activities. We actively foster and promote research and development in several areas of technology with focus on innovation, practicality and humanitarian principles. We also encourage dissemination of knowledge and has been instrumental in teaching students from India and abroad.

Our Vision

  • Our vision is to pioneer innovative technologies in precision tools that will enhance and simplify human life for a better tomorrow.

Our Mission

  • Our mission is to lead the industry by delivering exceptional service to our customers, fostering a dynamic and empowering environment that motivates our workforce, and making a positive impact on both the quality of life and sustainability of our planet..

Founder’s profile

Mr. Vishnu Vasudev Mujumdar was the founder of IAR Ballscrews and the Managing Director of the company. He had immense experience in the field of precision manufacturing. He started the Institute of Applied Research in 1980 IAR and since then, under his leadership, the company continued to grow and scale new peaks.

A man with a flair for entrepreneurship and an abundance of benevolence, he believed in contributing to the society in every way possible. His philanthropy and devotion to humanity made him the very essence of altruism. He believed that the purpose of technology and corporations should be for the upliftment of mankind, which has led to the initiation of many welfare programs. The company, under his guidance, actively encourages students globally to undertake industrial projects, enhancing their education.

A champion of environmental causes, he made efforts to employ sustainable techniques. From reducing the carbon footprint to using environmentally safe raw materials, he consistently strived to enhance the company's carbon footprint. Mr. Mujumdar embodied a rare combination of an entrepreneur, a technologist, and a humanitarian, continuing to inspire all with his work and vision.

“We are here to stay in the field of CNC and automation accessories. We are deeply devoted to the development of products and services. Our aim is complete customer satisfaction.”

V.V. Mujumdar,
Founder, IAR Ballscrews

Awards

IAR Ballscrews founder, Mr. V. V. Mujumdar, won several awards for his innovations and entrepreneurship.

1982

GS Parkhe Industrial Merit Award for Skid Rollers

1983

Gold medal for Cycloidal Wheel from the World Intellectual Property Organization

1983

Silver medal for Cycloidal Wheel from the Republic of France’s Institute of Industrial Property (Institut National De La Propriete Industrielle)

1983

ML Dahanukar Entrepreneurship Award

1983

GS Parkhe Industrial Merit Award for Multipurpose Simple Tool Bar & Bullock Cart with Flexible Wheels

1991

GS Parkhe Industrial Merit Award for Ball Lead Screw

Mr. Mujumdar served as the President of Alliance Francoise in Pune, India, and worked closely with the organization for 10 years. In 2013, Mr. Mujumdar was awarded Knight of Legion of Honour ─ France’s highest civilian award recognizing his services rendered to the country.
